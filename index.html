A smarter, sustainable way to keep cool on campus.

Coolock replaces noisy, wasteful personal mini-fridges with a sleek, shared, and secure solution for university halls.

Discover the Problem

WHY: The Problem with Personal Fridges

Fridges are a necessity, but the current model of one-per-student is inefficient, disruptive, and environmentally unsustainable.

Environmentally Damaging

Thousands of individual fridges create tons of e-waste, CO2 emissions, and landfill every year, consuming massive amounts of electricity.

Disruptive to Student Life

The constant hum is 40% over WHO guidelines for sleeping spaces. They take up precious room space and are a hassle to maintain and move.

Inefficient and Costly

Students pay for the fridge and its electricity, only to throw it away or sell it at a loss. It's a wasteful cycle, year after year.

WHAT: The Coolock Solution

A simple, secure, and fully-serviced shared refrigeration system designed from the ground up for the modern student.

Simple Pin-Code Access

No apps, no cards, no fuss. A simple pin is all you need. This means a seamless experience for you and no complex software for us to maintain.

Hassle-Free Maintenance

We handle everything. Coolock units are professionally cleaned and serviced every semester. No more defrosting a mini-fridge during finals week.

Conveniently Located

We install a Coolock unit in the pantry or kitchen area on each floor of the residence hall, right where you need it most.

HOW: A Win-Win Business Model

For Students

For just S$20/month—the same price as running a personal fridge—you get secure, hassle-free cooling without the burden of ownership. Save time, money, and space.

For the University

A zero-risk partnership. We manage everything from installation to electricity. This promotes campus sustainability and offers a new amenity for students.
