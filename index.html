A smarter, sustainable, and shared fridge for university life.

Say goodbye to the hassle of owning a fridge and hello to a cooler way to live on campus.

WHY: The Problem with Personal Fridges

Personal mini-fridges in dorm rooms are a source of significant environmental waste, noise pollution, and inconvenience for students. Fridges are a necessity, but the current model is broken.

Environmentally Damaging

Thousands of individual fridges on campus consume massive amounts of electricity and create tons of e-waste, CO2 emissions, and landfill every year.

Disruptive to Student Life

The constant 43 dB hum is 40% over WHO guidelines for sleep. They take up precious room space, create bad smells, and are a hassle to maintain, move, and dispose of.

Inefficient and Costly

Students pay for electricity and the fridge itself, only to throw it away or sell it at a loss. It's a wasteful cycle, year after year.

WHAT: Our Solution

Coolock is a shared, secure community fridge system designed for university dormitories. It's simple, efficient, and built for student life.

Simple Pin-Code Access

No apps, no cards, no fuss. A simple pin-code is all you need to access your personal locker. This means no complicated software for us to code or maintain, and a seamless experience for you.

Hassle-Free Maintenance

We handle everything. Coolock units are professionally cleaned and given a tune-up at the end of every semester. No more defrosting a mini-fridge during finals week.

Conveniently Located

We install and maintain a Coolock unit in the pantry or kitchen area on each floor of the residence hall, right where you need it.

HOW: The Business Model

For Students

For just S$20 per month, you get your own secure fridge locker. This is the same price students currently pay just for the electricity of an inefficient personal fridge. With Coolock, you get the convenience without the hassle of buying, moving, cleaning, or selling a fridge.

For the University

A risk-free partnership. We manage installation, maintenance, electricity costs, and student relations. The university can associate a fridge locker with certain rooms, similar to the existing model for air conditioning, creating a new revenue stream and promoting a sustainable campus.

WHO: Team & Partners

Our success relies on a strong network of customers, partners, and supporters working together to create a more sustainable campus life.

Our Customers

Our end-users are the 14,000 students living in NTU halls. Our key partners are the university and residence hall administrators who act as our middlemen.

Our Suppliers & Team

We partner with leading manufacturers like Hangzhou Dongcheng Electronics. Our operations team includes technicians, cleaning crews, and delivery drivers dedicated to a smooth service.

Our Supporters

We are seeking funding from NTU, government bodies, investment firms, and sustainability grants to launch and scale our vision.

Noise reduction in rooms by eliminating fridges that exceed WHO sleep guidelines.

Of landfill space avoided annually, the volume of five shipping containers.
